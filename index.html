Let's cook agile

by Vyacheslav Goldenshteyn

To create a masterpiece

You will need a dev-team.

Let's take our dev-team

fill half of it with Scrum

and put it on a project reach the boiling point.

Meanwhile let's take our features

slice it thin

as thin as a userstory could be.

Chewing gum helps our eyes to stay dry.

Now let's add sliced userstories to our backlog

and leave it for a while to boil.

At the same time we take pair programming

some TDD

and whip it with our tools

until it reaches

cloudy-cream CI.

Let's digress and leave with our devops to heat up a bit.

Continuing whipping

until reaching a smooth color

of CD.

Now it's time to leave all infrastructure in charge of devops.

Now we can take some rest.

And now it's time to

grind architecture with refactoring.

The only thing left is to add

some time-tracking

brown-bag meetings

and a pinch of teambuilding to make it taste better.

So, our dish is ready.

Should be brought up with an experienced manager.

Hurry up, before it gets cold! ;)
