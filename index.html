Let's cook agile

by Vyacheslav Goldenshteyn

To create a masterpiece

You will need a devteam.

Let's take our devteam

fill half of it with Scrum

and put it on a project reach the boiling point.

Meanwhile let's take our Problem

slice it thin

as thin as userstory could be.

Chewing gum will help our eyes to stay dry.

Now let's add sliced userstories to our project

and leave it for a while to boil.

At the same time we take pair programming

some TDD

and whip it with our tools

until it reaches

cloudy-cream continuous integration.

Let's digress and leave our devops team to heat up a bit.

Continuing whipping

until reaching a smooth colour

of continuous delivery.

Now it's time to leave all infrastructure in devop's team.

Now we can take some rest.

And now it's time to

grind architecture with refractoring.

The only thing left is to add

some timetracking

BDD

and a pinch of teambuilding to make it taste better.

So, our dish is ready.

It should be brought up with a experienced manager.

Hurry up, until it gets cold! ;)
